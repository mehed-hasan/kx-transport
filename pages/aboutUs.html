Full-range IT-Enabled

KX Transports is full-range IT-enabled. From collecting and processing data to generating business intelligence and making decisions, IT is stitched deep in KX Transports operations to harvest the best of the latest technology and bring accuracy, speed & infallible agility.

Customer-Centric

We are obsessed with our customers’ needs. We’ve organized our services in the most customer-friendly way so our clients always consider us an extension of their businesses.

Fast & Responsible

KX Transports has made it a mission to provide fast deliveries responsibly reducing cancellation ration in order to boost the business of our clients.

Extensive Tracking

Our hi-tech tracking system enables our customers to access a total overview of their parcels at any point in time before the delivery.

Digital Delivery Validation

One-time password (OTP) based delivery process allows us to keep a digital signature of every delivery, which is automatically sorted and uploaded to the system making the whole process secure and fast.

Cash on Delivery (COD)

COD services allow us to collect money from the customers, we maintain standard banking procedures for transferring the money to our clients.

Comprehensive Return Options

We have a simple and extensive return policy so that customers can return the delivered products without any hassle.

Live Delivery Associate Tracking

Our digital LDT allows you to live-track our delivery associates using your smartphones. Just log into KX Transports mobile app and do a search using your order number.
