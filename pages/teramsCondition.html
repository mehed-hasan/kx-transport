What are cookies?

Cookies are generally small and removable computerized pieces of information that provide KX Transports with a better insight into its user, visitor or customer by accessing browsing history and preferences regarding the website. The small text file is stored in the location of the user’s devices (desktop, laptop, tablets, smartphones or other devices from where the website is surfed). Cookies allow the sender to recognize the device in which it is kept throughout its validity terms. We also use other technological measures like web beacons and HTML5 local storage. This will help us to improve our website and provide users with a better experience and more customized results. You have the privilege to accept or reject cookies, web beacons and html5 local storage.
